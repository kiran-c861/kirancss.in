ANIMAL
The elephant is the largest land animal, known for its intelligence, strong social bonds, and incredible memory. Found in Africa and Asia, elephants have large ears, long trunks, and tusks (in some species). They are herbivores, consuming grass, fruits, and bark. Sadly, they face threats from poaching and habitat loss.
